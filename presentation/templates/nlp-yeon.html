Natural Language Processing on News Headlines to see how they affect stock price¶

Why is what you build relevant and what problem does it help solve?¶

You can get a sense of how news headlines are correlated to stock prices.

Who benefits from what you built?¶

Anyone looking for some reference to analyze stock market, this can be used as a starter.

How much are you assuming I already know in order to use your tool?¶

Reader has some understanding and interest for stock market and some knowledge of Natural Language Processing, Vader algorithm to be specific, and can determine outlier scores on his own discretion

import requests
target_url = "https://newsapi.org/v2/everything?domains=nytimes.com&q=amazon&from=2018-01-01&to=2018-02-07&language=en&pageSize=100&apiKey=09b743275f1d407e8a4f1dc4cb46677d"
data = requests.get(target_url).json()['articles']

from vaderSentiment.vaderSentiment import SentimentIntensityAnalyzer
analyzer = SentimentIntensityAnalyzer()

news_data = {
    "title": [],
    "scores": [],
    "dates": []
}
for headline in data:
    title = headline["title"]
    news_data["title"].append(title)
    news_data["scores"].append(analyzer.polarity_scores(title)["compound"])
    news_data["dates"].append(headline["publishedAt"])

news_df = pd.DataFrame(news_data, columns=["scores","dates","title"])
news_df
